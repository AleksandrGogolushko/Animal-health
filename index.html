our clinic

Our friendly team of veterinary healthcare professionals believes in providing quality and affordable care for all animals great and small. Is a full service animal hospital and will take both emergency cases as well as less urgent medical, surgical, orthopedic and dental issues and we also have our own in-house lab. Our veterinarians are experienced in all types of conditions and treatments. about us

Animal Health Clinic is a full-service animal veterinary hospital. The facility includes a fully-stocked pharmacy, surgery suite, in-house x-ray, a closely monitored hospitalization area, and boarding for cats, with an outdoor yard for walking hospitalized dogs. We provide a broad spectrum of diagnostic procedures through in-house testing and the use of external laboratories. Our hospital is dedicated to animal welfare and preventative medicine for your pets. Labwork, radiographs, exams, etc may be performed in our clinic and emailed or faxed to your specialists to save you a trip with your pet.
